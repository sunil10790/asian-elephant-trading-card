Asian Elephant

Elephants go through 6 sets of teeth in their lifetimes. Other mammals have 2.

  • Scientific name: Elephas maximus
  • Lifespan: 48 years
  • Mass: 5,400 kg
  • Height: 2.7 m

The Asian or Asiatic elephant is distributed in Southeast Asia from India and Nepal in the west to Borneo in the east. Asian elephants are the largest living land animals in Asia.
